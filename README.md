# 🎤⛓️ Crypto Bars — Cryptocurrency in Rap Lyrics

A verified catalog of moments where **rap and hip-hop lyrics name-check cryptocurrency** — Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana, Dogecoin, blockchain, Coinbase, "crypto," HODL and friends.

Crypto is young (Bitcoin's genesis block: January 3, 2009), so this is a finite, traceable slice of culture: you can watch digital money travel from cypherpunk forums into novelty crypto-rap (YTCracker, 2013), into a stray bar on an underground record (Action Bronson, 2014), and finally into the mouths of Drake, Kendrick, and Baby Keem on chart records. This repo tries to map that drift, carefully.

## How this was built (and what "verified" means)

Every entry's lyric was **confirmed verbatim against a fetchable, citable source** (a news article, a label/lyrics page, or an artist's own post) before it made the table. Nothing here is from memory or guesswork — if a line couldn't be sourced, it didn't ship. References that are *reported but couldn't be independently verified* are quarantined in a [separate section](#-reported-but-not-verified) rather than mixed in.

A few honesty notes:

- **Genius links go to a Genius *search* query**, not a direct lyric-page URL. Genius blocks automated access, so direct page slugs couldn't be programmatically validated — a search link is guaranteed to resolve and lands you on the right page.
- **YouTube links** were pulled from search results. Most are official audio/video; a couple are the best available upload. Found a wrong link? [Open an issue](../../issues).
- `(partial)` marks an excerpt where fewer than ~4 contiguous bars could be sourced. Lines are quoted as the source rendered them.
- **Confidence:** `High` = lyric confirmed on a strong/primary source. `Medium` = confirmed via a single secondary source or search snippets.

## Notable crypto people who *don't* rap about it (on record)

Plenty of artists are deep in crypto as **investors or businesspeople** but have no in-lyric reference: **Jay-Z** (Bitcoin Academy), **50 Cent** (sold *Animal Ambition* for BTC), **Megan Thee Stallion** (#BitcoinMeg giveaways), **Logic** ($6M BTC buy), **Jim Jones** (Capo Coin), **DDG**, **Gunna** (PushinPETH), **2 Chainz**, **Cardi B**, **Kanye West**. Owning the bag isn't the same as putting it in a bar — and this catalog only counts the bars.
